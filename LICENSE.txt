Player by HEIMLICH® - Professional Video Player
Professional video player for media analysis and frame-by-frame inspection

Features:
• Frame-by-frame navigation
• Professional video analysis tools
• High-resolution video support
• Advanced playback controls
• Multi-format support (MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, WMV, etc.)
• Customizable interface
• Real-time video scopes and analysis

System Requirements:
• Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
• DirectX 11 compatible graphics card
• 4GB RAM minimum, 8GB recommended
• 500MB free disk space for installation

For support and updates, visit:
https://github.com/heimlich/player-by-heimlich
